## TL;DR
- ~70–75% of traffic is **direct** → weak SEO-driven discovery  
- 90% of users land on **homepage only** → limited internal navigation  
- Exit rates reach **~80–90%** → low engagement depth  
- Blog contributes **~1%** → not yet a traffic acquisition channel  

👉 **Decision:** Data is sufficient to proceed with immediate SEO and website optimization — delaying for more data would not change the outcome.

---

## Overview
This case study analyzes real-world traffic data from an enterprise website to evaluate SEO visibility, user behavior, and content performance during its early growth stage.

The objective is to determine whether the available dataset is sufficient to support immediate optimization decisions and to identify the highest-impact areas for improving organic discovery and user engagement.

Key components include:
- Problem definition and dataset validation  
- Traffic acquisition and SEO visibility analysis  
- User behavior and engagement patterns  
- Data-supported and hypothesis-driven recommendations  
- Business impact and decision-making outcomes
